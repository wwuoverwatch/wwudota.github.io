We Are The Vikingwatch

The Vikingwatch is Western Washington University's official Overwatch club. Members are invited to join our Discord and Facebook to ask questions, organize fun events, and keep our community connected. If you're looking to play with other club members, hop onto our Discord server and see who's down to party up. We're a friendly bunch, but if someone is misbehaving let an officer know.

Meetings & Events

Official club games are currently played online and are typically organized through Discord. Official club meetings will start at the beginning of Winter quarter 2017.

Club officers will organize one in-person event per quarter but we also encourage club members to organize events as well.
